What to Do

By Destination

May 9–12
Amalfi

3 nights · Cliffs, coast & lemon groves

  • Duomo di Sant'Andrea — 9th-century cathedral, Cloister of Paradise, crypt of St Andrew. A few minutes' walk from the hotel
  • Ravello — Villa Cimbrone's Terrace of Infinity, Villa Rufolo gardens. ~25 min by car above Amalfi
  • Positano — Pastel-cliff village. Best reached by SITA ferry from Amalfi (~25 min)
  • Path of the Gods — Iconic 7km ridge hike, Bomerano → Nocelle, panoramic coast views, ~3h
  • Capri day trip — Fast ferry from Amalfi (~50 min). Anacapri, Monte Solaro chairlift, Blue Grotto if seas allow
  • Emerald Grotto (Conca dei Marini) — Light-dappled sea cave, 4km west of Amalfi
  • Museo della Carta — Paper museum in Amalfi's Valle delle Ferriere. Easy riverside walk behind town
  • Atrani — Quieter village next door, 10-min walk via the footpath
  • Dei Cappuccini — Acclaimed on-site restaurant at Anantara, former refectory with sea view (not Michelin-starred per 2026 Guide)
  • La Caravella — 1 Michelin star (since 1959, oldest in Southern Italy), majolica dining room, exceptional wine list · 5-min walk from hotel
  • Il Flauto di Pan — 1 Michelin star at Villa Cimbrone, Ravello; ~8 min taxi up the hill, evenings only, 35 seats
  • Rossellinis — 1 Michelin star at Palazzo Avino, Ravello; dramatic terrace over the Gulf of Salerno
  • La Sponda at Le Sirenuse — 1 Michelin star, 400 candles, Positano's iconic evening (25 min ferry + late taxi back)
May 12–16
Venice

4 nights · Biennale & beyond

  • Biennale: Arsenale — "In Minor Keys" main exhibition, 111 artists, allow 4–5 hours. 11am–7pm (Fri/Sat until 8pm)
  • Biennale: Giardini — 30 national pavilions (US, UK, France, Japan, Germany). Closed Mondays
  • Collateral events — 31 satellite shows across Venice
  • Peggy Guggenheim — Pollock, Picasso, Ernst. Open daily 10–18 except Tuesdays. Visit Wed or Thu morning
  • La Fenice: Carmen — Bizet's opera, May 14 evening. Book tickets early at teatrolafenice.it
  • Dorsoduro & Castello — Quieter sestieri. Scala Contarini del Bovolo for spiral staircase views
  • Antiche Carampane — Outstanding Venetian seafood (May 12, 9:30pm ✓)
  • Alle Testiere — 10 tables, extraordinary seafood tasting (May 13, 9:30pm ✓)
  • Da Fiore — Michelin-starred, legendary risotto (May 14, 7pm ✓)
  • Glam by Enrico Bartolini — 2 Michelin stars at Palazzo Venart. Creative tasting menu (May 15, 7:30pm — to book)
May 16–18
Lake Como

2 nights · Villas, gardens & ferries

  • Villa Balbianello — Open daily except Mon & Wed, 10–18. Book at fondoambiente.it. Sun May 17 ✓
  • Villa Carlotta — Azaleas & rhododendrons in peak bloom. Open 10–19, last ticket 18:00
  • Villa Monastero — 2km botanical garden, lakeside, Varenna
  • Villa Melzi — Japanese pond, sculptures, Bellagio
  • Golden Triangle — Varenna → Bellagio → Menaggio by ferry. Buy day pass
  • Private boat tour — Wooden boat past famous villas incl. Villa Oleandra (Clooney's). ~€240/hr
  • Greenway del Lago — Gentle 10km cycling/walk along western shore. E-bike rental available
  • Castello di Vezio — 20-min hike, panoramic castle above Varenna
  • Al Prato — Varenna's best, 7pm May 16. Hotel private boat across the lake at sunset & return after dinner
  • L'Escale Trattoria — Grand Hotel Tremezzo (May 16, 7pm ✓)
  • Visteria — Grand Hotel Tremezzo (May 17, 7pm ✓)
May 18–19

Amalfi Dining

Hotel & Restaurants

Our hotel sits on the cliff above Amalfi town, a 5-minute walk down to Piazza del Duomo. Michelin dining clusters in three zones: Amalfi town (walkable), Ravello (short taxi up the hill), and Positano (25 min by ferry, or a pre-booked return taxi after the ferry stops around 8 pm).

Il Flauto di Pan

Positano · 25 min ferry / 45 min taxi
1 Michelin star; 400-candle dining room. Book weeks ahead. Ferry back stops ~8 pm — pre-book a return taxi (~45 min, €100+). Smart casual, no shorts; jackets optional.
