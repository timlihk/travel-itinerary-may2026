What to Do

By Destination

May 9–11
Geneva

3 nights · Lakeside & culture

  • Quai du Mont-Blanc — Chestnut trees in bloom, Jet d'Eau, afternoon promenade
  • Patek Philippe Museum — Four floors of horological masterpieces. Closed Mon. Open Sat 10–18, Sun 14–18. Note: May 9 exceptional closure 10am–2pm, visit after 14:00
  • Bains des Pâquis — Iconic lake baths, casual fondue. Open 10–21 (Sun from 9). Morning swim for best light
  • Vieille Ville — St. Peter's Cathedral tower, Maison Tavel, hidden courtyards
  • CERN Science Gateway — Free immersive exhibition. Open Tue–Sun 9–17. Closed Mondays. Book timed entry online
  • Mont Salève cable car — Panoramic views of lake & Alps. Half-day trip, accessible by bus
  • Carouge — Geneva's "little Italy," artisan streets, Saturday market, La Bottega trattoria
  • Il Lago at Four Seasons — Michelin-starred lakeside Italian (Sat May 9, 7pm ✓)
  • L'Arabesque at President Wilson — Lebanese, replacing Bayview (closed Sun/Mon) (Sun May 10, 7pm ✓)
  • Café du Soleil — Legendary Geneva fondue
May 12–16
Venice

4 nights · Biennale & beyond

  • Biennale: Arsenale — "In Minor Keys" main exhibition, 111 artists, allow 4–5 hours. 11am–7pm (Fri/Sat until 8pm)
  • Biennale: Giardini — 30 national pavilions (US, UK, France, Japan, Germany). Closed Mondays
  • Collateral events — 31 satellite shows across Venice
  • Peggy Guggenheim — Pollock, Picasso, Ernst. Open daily 10–18 except Tuesdays. Visit Wed or Thu morning
  • La Fenice: Carmen — Bizet's opera, May 14 evening. Book tickets early at teatrolafenice.it
  • Dorsoduro & Castello — Quieter sestieri. Scala Contarini del Bovolo for spiral staircase views
  • Antiche Carampane — Outstanding Venetian seafood (May 12, 9:30pm ✓)
  • Alle Testiere — 10 tables, extraordinary seafood tasting (May 13, 9:30pm ✓)
  • Da Fiore — Michelin-starred, legendary risotto (May 14, 7pm ✓)
  • Glam by Enrico Bartolini — 2 Michelin stars at Palazzo Venart. Creative tasting menu (May 15, 7:30pm — to book)
May 16–18
Lake Como

2 nights · Villas, gardens & ferries

  • Villa Balbianello — Open daily except Mon & Wed, 10–18. Book at fondoambiente.it. Sun May 17 ✓
  • Villa Carlotta — Azaleas & rhododendrons in peak bloom. Open 10–19, last ticket 18:00
  • Villa Monastero — 2km botanical garden, lakeside, Varenna
  • Villa Melzi — Japanese pond, sculptures, Bellagio
  • Golden Triangle — Varenna → Bellagio → Menaggio by ferry. Buy day pass
  • Private boat tour — Wooden boat past famous villas incl. Villa Oleandra (Clooney's). ~€240/hr
  • Greenway del Lago — Gentle 10km cycling/walk along western shore. E-bike rental available
  • Castello di Vezio — 20-min hike, panoramic castle above Varenna
  • Al Prato — Varenna's best, 7pm May 16. Hotel private boat across the lake at sunset & return after dinner
  • L'Escale Trattoria — Grand Hotel Tremezzo (May 16, 7pm ✓)
  • Visteria — Grand Hotel Tremezzo (May 17, 7pm ✓)
May 19–20
Dublin

2 nights · Business & culture

  • Book of Kells & Old Library — Trinity College. Pre-book early entry at tcd.ie
  • St Patrick's Cathedral & St Stephen's Green — Historic Dublin walk
  • National Gallery of Ireland — Free, excellent Caravaggio
  • Restaurant Patrick Guilbaud — 2 Michelin stars, at The Merrion (our hotel)
  • The Long Hall — Victorian pub, Guinness done right
